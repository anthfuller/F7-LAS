# Contributor Code of Conduct

This project follows a simple rule:
**Be respectful, constructive, and aligned with the purpose of F7-LAS.**

## Behavior Expectations
- Treat all contributors with respect.
- Provide helpful and actionable feedback.
- Avoid personal attacks or dismissive language.
- Keep discussions focused on technical and conceptual topics.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment or threats.
- Spam, marketing, or vendor solicitation.
- Submitting unverifiable or unsafe code.
- Attempting to derail or dominate conversations.

## Reporting
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the repository maintainer.

## Enforcement
The maintainer may:
- Provide warnings  
- Close or reject PRs  
- Remove issues or comments  
- Ban repeat offenders  

Thank you for helping maintain a healthy, productive environment.
